Kyle Silver

Flight (for String Quartet)

A short, light piece that plays with fast and irregular time signatures. I originally wrote this piece in 2011 and revisited it ten years later to reflect what I’ve learned in the intervening years. When I was originally writing this in Noteflight I had a lot of fun with the transposition tool, and the result was a preponderance of ascending sequences, hence: “Flight”.

You can view a PDF of the full score here. Parts are available on request.

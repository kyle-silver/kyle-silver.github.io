Kyle Silver

Flight (for String Quartet)

A short, light piece that plays with fast and irregular time signatures. I originally wrote this piece in 2011 and revisited it ten years later to reflect on what I’ve learned in the intervening years. Final verdict: my part-writing has improved significantly; my melodic creativity is just as inconsistent as ever. Oh well…

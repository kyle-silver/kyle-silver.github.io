Too Good Too Bad (Cowboy Bebop)

Not a whole lot to say here, just a solid minute of absolutely killing it over the Chameleon bassline. This was always one of my favorite tracks from the first companion soundtrack, and I’m happy I could finally transcribe it. There is one bit of mystery here, which is that I can’t find definitive attribution for who actually performed the solo. Masato Honda is very well known for his solo on the album’s opener Tank! but I can’t say for certain that he’s the soloist here.
